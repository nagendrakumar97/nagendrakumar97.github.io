prasatavana

“Prasatavana” (Paper Presentation) a perfect platform for the under-graduate to come out with their innovative ideas, and present your innate talents in the form of technical paper presentations.

❖ TOPICS :

 Nonconventional Energy Sources
 HVDC & FACTS
 Advanced Control Systems
 Modern trends in Electric Drives
 Microprocessors and Microcontrollers
 Any other advanced topic in Electrical Engineering

❖ INSTRUCTIONS :

✧Maximum Two Participants/ Paper are allowed.
✧Each team has to send the soft copy of full length paper to exult@pragati.ac.in with     subject as Paper Presentation.
✧You may send the hard copy of your paper to “CONVENER-STRIDES 2k17”, Pragati     Engineering College, # 1-378 , ADB Road, Surampalem, E.G.District,    A.P.-533 437” ,     so as to reach us on or before 31.12.2016
✧A full length paper should in IEEE format (in two column) not exceeding the 5 to 6     papers.
✧Team should mention their College name, Branch name, Name of the participants     and their contact number with email ids on the first page of word file.
✧Presentation slide count should not exceed 15.
✧Duration of presentation is 8 minutes and 2 minutes for query session.
✧Last Date for Full length paper submission : 31.12.2016
✧Last Date for Intimation of the selected papers : 02.01.2017
✧Date of the National Level Technical Symposium: 09.01.2017
✧Selected participants should bring the Respective College ID Cards/Bonafide     Certificate from the Head of their Institution.
✧Registration for the selected papers will be done at the Venue.
